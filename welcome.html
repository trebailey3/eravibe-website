Why I built this

I wanted a social app
I could be proud of.

"What are you into right now? That one question tells me more about you than any grid of curated photos ever could."

Ever since social networking started, yes I'm that old, I've watched it throughout the years hollow out real connection. Everyone became hyper focused on follower counts and how many likes did my recent post get. Metrics that make people feel like they're not enough, creating anxiety, depression, and that addiction to "go viral" or become an influencer. Algorithms designed to keep you scrolling. Ads disguised as content. I didn't want to build any of that. Just the opposite.

I created er@ with a simple belief: your taste is your identity. I want to know the shows you're watching, the music you can't stop listening to, the places you've been or plan to visit, everything that tells me who the real you is. Not your highlight reel. Not your follower count. Not how many likes your last post got.

So I built er@ with that as my driving force, to help us connect on a deeper level with one another. Three active pins — the things you're into right now, with photos and captions to capture the moment. Photos that expire in 72 hours so you never feel trapped by your past. And when you're done with a chapter, you can swap a pin, create a new chapter, and archive that moment of your life into a named era. A genuine record of who you were during that time. Your profile becomes your biography, your archived eras become your digital scrapbook, and your life is real, genuine, not a performance.

For friends and family, it means something real: I actually know what you're into. That shared obsession or mutual interest is the start of real connection. I hope you love the app as much as I do and it brings you happiness and the joy of connecting again on a deeper level, as social media was meant to create initially. Where it failed, we hope to succeed. Enjoy.

— Tony

Privacy & Safety

Protection is never
paywalled here.

Every privacy and safety feature in er@ is free. We don't sell protection as a premium. er@+ is purely about self-expression upgrades: add a 4th pin for greater expression, video posts with longer photo & video expiry, custom era theme colors when archiving. Your safety is not a product you need to purchase, ever.

Follow Control

You approve every follow

All follows require your explicit approval — on by default, always. You can see a requester's current pins before you decide. Either party can remove a follow relationship at any time. Auto-approve is available as an option for users 18 and older.

Comments

Three-tier comment control

Delete any comment instantly. Restrict someone from commenting while keeping them as a follower. Or remove them entirely. Global off is always available. Per-post settings at the moment of posting.

Your Data

We collect only what we need

Your handle, email, pins, eras, photos, and captions. Device type and OS for performance. Crash logs and aggregated usage events. That's the complete list.

Legal

COPPA & GDPR compliant

Minimum age 13. Ages 13–17 require parent or guardian review. Consent timestamps stored on every user record. Right to access, correct, delete, and export your data — always.

Contacts

We never store your contacts

When you search for friends, your contact list is hashed for matching only, then immediately discarded. We never store, read, or message your contacts.

Photos

Real expiry, not just hidden

Active photos are deleted from our servers within 30 days of their expiry — not just hidden from view. Era archive thumbnails are retained while your account is active.

Screenshots

Screenshots are blocked

To protect the privacy of our users, er@ blocks screenshots within the app. What someone shares on er@ stays on er@ — it can't be captured and taken elsewhere without their knowledge.

Content Safety

Sensitive content is blocked

er@ actively screens for and blocks sensitive, harmful, and explicit content. If something slips through, users can report abuse or flag any user directly from their profile or post. Every report is reviewed.

How we make money.

We believe you deserve to know exactly how a social app sustains itself. Here's every revenue stream we have or plan to build — nothing hidden.

StreamWhat it isWhat it means for you
er@+ Subscription $3.99/mo or $29.99/yr. Unlocks a 4th pin, 7-day photo expiry, custom era themes, and er@+ badge. 7-day free trial on your first subscription. The main way we earn. You pay us directly. No advertiser involved.

Affiliate Commerce When we recommend a product and you buy it, we earn a small commission (typically 5–15%). Moment Partnerships future Brands sponsor cultural pin events — a limited-time pin category tied to a real cultural moment. Think a film studio sponsoring a "movie era" pin during a major release. These will be disclosed, limited, and tied to cultural moments you'd likely be into anyway. Never interrupting your feed.
Taste Graph Licensing future Anonymized, aggregated trend data — what pins are surging, what's going viral in real taste — sold to music labels, studios, and brands. This data is never tied to you personally. It's population-level trend intelligence — like a music chart, not a personal profile. We will never sell individual user data.

Our commitment, in plain English:

We will never run third-party ads. We will never sell your personal data — that means your name, email, photos, pins, or anything tied to you as an individual. We will never build an algorithm designed to maximize your time on screen at the expense of your mental health. Every dollar we earn comes from you choosing to support us, or from brands paying for cultural relevance — not from selling your attention.
