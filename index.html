how it works

your identity is what
you love right now

Not your follower count. Not a curated grid. Just 3 things you're obsessed with — photos that prove it, and a shop powered by everything you love.

01
Set 3 pins

Name 3 things you're into right now. A show, a song, a place, a feeling. No categories. No rules. Just what's real.

02
Post to a pin

Take a photo. Tag it to one of your pins. It disappears in 72 hours. Low stakes means you actually post what's real.

03
Find your people

Discover who else has the same pins. Taste is a better way to find your people than location or mutual friends.

04
Start a new era

When you move on, swap a pin. Your photos freeze into a named era — a chapter of your life, archived forever.
